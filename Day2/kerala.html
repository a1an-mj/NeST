Kerala

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a top tourist destination known for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, and beautiful beaches. Visitors can explore tranquil houseboat cruises along the backwaters of Alleppey and Kumarakom, relax on the beaches of Kovalam and Varkala, or trek through the Western Ghats in Munnar and Wayanad. The state is also famous for its Ayurvedic wellness retreats, vibrant cultural festivals like Onam, and rich history.
